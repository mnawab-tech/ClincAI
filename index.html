The Feynman Technique: explain it simply, find gaps, go back and refine. Best for complex clinical AI concepts.

Step 1 — Explain it simply

Write as if you're teaching a 12-year-old. No jargon.

Step 2 — Identify gaps

What did you struggle to explain? What terms felt unclear?

Step 3 — Refined explanation

After reviewing your gaps, write a clearer version.

Step 4 — Analogy

Connect it to something familiar from medicine or everyday life.
